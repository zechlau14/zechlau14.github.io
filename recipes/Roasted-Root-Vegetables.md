## Ingredients

* 4  carrots

* 1 red onion

* 2 sweet potato

* 3 tbsp olive oil

* 1.5 tbsp apple cider or balsamic vinegar

* 1 tsp dried herbs. e.g. thyme, rosemary or sage

* 1/2 tsp salt

* 1/2 tsp pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 220C

2. Toss vegetables with oil,vinegar, herbs, salt and pepper.

3. Roast vegetables for 30-40 min.
