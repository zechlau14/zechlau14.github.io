## Ingredients

* 1.5 cups of rice
* 50g lotus roots
* 50g carrots
* 2-3 shiitake mushrooms
* 1.5 cups of dashi stock
* 1 tbsp sake
* 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
* 1/2 tsp sugar

## Instructions

1. Slice lotus roots, carrots and shiitake.
2. Cook all the ingredients into a rice cooker.
