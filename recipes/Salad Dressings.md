## Herb Vinaigrette

* 1/3 cup olive oil

* 1/3 cup vinegar

* 1 tbsp dried herbs

* 2 tbsp diced shallots

* 2 tbsp lemon juice

* 1/4 tsp salt

* 1/4 tsp pepper

## Vinaigrette with Mustard

* 6 tbsp olive oil

* 3 tbsp red-wine vinegar

* 1.5 tbsp diced shallots

* 1 tbsp Dijon mustard

* 2/3 tbsp dried thyme

* 1/4 tsp salt

* 1/2 tsp pepper

## Chilli lime dressing

* 2 tbsp lime juice

* 1 tsp sugar

* 1 red chilli

* 2 tsp soy sauce

## Fish sauce and lime dressing

* 1/4 cup lime juice

* 2 tbsp brown sugar

* 2 tbsp fish sauce

## Lemon Vinaigrette

* 1/3 cup olive oil
* 1/4 cup lemon juice
* 1 tsp Dijon mustard
* 1/2 tsp honey
* 1 clove minced garlic
* salt and pepper

## Caesar Salad Dressing
* 2 cloves garlic
* 2 tsp dijon mustard
* 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
* 2 tsp lemon juice
* 1.5 tsp red wine vinegar
* 1/3 cup olive oil
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/8 tsp black pepper
* 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
