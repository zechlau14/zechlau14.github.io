## Ingredients
* 100g butter
* 150g icing sugar
* 30g cocoa powder
* pinch of salt
* 25ml milk (or water)

## Instructions
1. Beat butter on high for 30 seconds until light.
2. Add the rest of the ingredients, and mix on low speed until creamy.
*Note*: If frosting seems dry, add additional liquid.
